TENNIS GAME





INTRODUCTION🚩

This game simulates a tennis match, aiming to add fun while exercising your reaction ability. Would you accept the tennis match with Cake Cat?

  • 1. You need to hold your tennis racket with the mouse and not release it before dragging the tennis racket.

  • 2. When you are ready to start, click "start serve" to begin the game.

  • 3. Remember to turn on the computer volume, but not too high.🎶

PLAY
